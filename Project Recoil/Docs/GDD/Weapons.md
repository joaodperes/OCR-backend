# Weapon Philosophy
Every weapon is viable.

There are no direct upgrades.

Unlocking a weapon gives the player more choices, not more power.

# Every weapon has a purpose.

Instead of

```
Assault Rifle #1
Assault Rifle #2
```

we ask

> Why would a player intentionally choose this?

# Stats are trade-offs.

Good example:

Higher damage
↓
More recoil
↓
Slower reload
↓
Smaller magazine

Never:

Higher damage
Higher accuracy
Faster reload
Larger magazine

# Every weapon has a personality.

Players should remember weapons because of how they feel, not just their damage numbers.
